Lukas Koch


I'm a 4th year math PhD student at the University of Oxford under the supervision of Professor Jan Kristensen; I'm also a stipendiary lecturer at St Hilda's College, Oxford .

My research is mostly concerned with regularity questions in the Calculus of Variations. One area of focus have been global regularity results for functionals with p-q-growth. A second area I am currently interested in is existence of solutions to the Jacobian equation with low-regularity data.

My email address is kochl at maths.ox.ac.uk.
